 HNG Internship Program
 Hng Internship Program is all about making you to be the best in your area of interest,
 by setting a  bridge between learning to code, and becoming the best in the world.
 The internship is designed to build your confidence in the real world.
During the 3 months period, the interns are exposed to several practices obtainable in the tech industries, modern developer tools, rapid application development among a host of others. This is achieved by providing real products to be built under strict supervision of highly experienced software Engineers
